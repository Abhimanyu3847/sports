Gentleman's Game

Cricket is a bat-and-ball contest celebrated for its strategic depth and dramatic finishes. From village greens to colossal stadiums, the sound of leather on willow creates moments that linger for generations. Discover the patience, precision and passion that make cricket a timeless spectacle.

Top Competitions

ICC Cricket World Cup

The premier international championship where nations compete for the ultimate prize in 50-over cricket.

ICC T20 World Cup

The fastest format's global showdown, delivering edge-of-the-seat action in just twenty overs per side.

Indian Premier League

A glamorous franchise tournament blending world-class talent with high entertainment and electric atmospheres.

Legendary Players

Sachin Tendulkar

Master batsman with impeccable technique and unbreakable concentration, holder of one hundred international centuries.

Vivian Richards

Devastating batter who dominated bowlers without a helmet, embodying swagger and consistency across eras.

MS Dhoni

Cool-headed finisher and master tactician, guiding India to World Cup glory with calm nerves and lightning stumpings.
